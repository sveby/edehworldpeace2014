Very Rev. Fr Prof. Emmanuel M.P. Edeh

MADONNA UNIVERSITY NIGERIA

  1. Articulation of African Philosophy of Being in which he developed the concept of man as "mma-di" (good that is) showing that peace is innate in man who is created and cared for by God, the ultimate Good. Man in reciprocity must care for fellow man thereby bringing peace to the modern world.
  2. Founder of the famous Centre for Peace Justice and Reconciliation which has settled over 14 million cases free of charge.
  3. Established Medical Health Care Institutions to bring healthcare to thousands in the society who cannot pay for medical services. Example: Madonna University Teaching Hospital Elele (MUTH), Our Saviour Hospital Elele, Medical Clinics in Okija, Akpugo, Enugu etc.
  4. Established Umuogbenye Centre Elele when abjectly poor and helpless people are properly fed and cared.
  5. Rehabilitation Centre established 1986 where people are rehabilitated, introduced into self help projects and trained in various skills like making bread, pomade, wine, soap etc.
  6. Established the famous Madonna International Charity Peace Award (MICPA) in 2006.
  7. Educational establishments - Viz: Nursery and Primary: a) 1986 - Our Saviour International School Elele,; b) 1997-Elizabeth Model Akpugo,;c) 1990-Our Saviour, Aba.;d) 1998 - Madonna Model Abuja; e) 2007 - Caritas Ibadan. Tertiary Institutions: a) 1989 - OSISATECH Polytechnic Enugu; b) 1989 - OSISATECH College of Education; c)1993 - Madonna University Nigeria; d)2004 - Caritas University Enugu.
  8. Several awards conferred on Very Rev. Fr. Prof. Emmanuel M.P. Edeh C.S.Sp, OFR for example:
    a) "Ambassador for Peace" by Federation Inter Religious and International Federation for World Peace.
    b) "Rev. Fr. Edeh Day" Sunday September 24th 2006 proclaimed by Mayor of City of Miami Gardens Florida, USA.
    c) ZAFAA awards in London for his enormous contributions to Peace and Charity in Africa and beyond…
  9. Established the Museum of Charms (Voodoo) and Fetish Objects by which he collects and locked up millions of objects and evil creation used by bad people to harm fellow humans in various parts of the world thereby freeing millions of people from evil attacks thereby enabling peace to rain in the hearts of millions in this modern world.
  10. In the effort to perpetuate his Mission of Practical and Effective Charity, empowering millions of the youths through proper education, enabling the sick, abjectly poor and abandoned to have peace through all his establishments.
  11. Very Rev. Fr. Prof. Emmanuel M.P. Edeh C.S.Sp, OFR Founded four religious families, namely: a) The Sisters of Jesus the Saviour; b) Fathers of Jesus the Saviour; c) Male Contemplatives of Jesus the Saviour; d) Female Contemplatives of Jesus the Saviour.
  12. Through The National Pilgrimage Elele, Nigeria which is the largest in Africa and the 5th in the whole Christian World, PEACE continuously pured in the hearts of thousands and millions of pilgrims who flock in the Centre on daily, weekly and monthly basis.
  13. It is important to note that the eight point's agenda of the Millennium Developments Goals (MDGs) were already being fully implemented by Fr. Prof. Edeh almost 15 years ever before they were declared by the United Nations in 1998.
